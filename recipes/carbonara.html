Carbonara

  • Grab 120g spagetthi, 3 eggs, half a pack of tulip diced bacon, and some pecorino cheese.
  • Boil water, add salt.
  • Add the spaghetti, set a timer for 8 minutes.
  • Heat a steel skillet, add olive oil, then the bacon.
  • Add one whole egg in a bowl and two yokes, and the pecorino cheese
  • Add some freshly ground pepper
  • When the spaghetti is done, add some of the water from boiling it to the bowl, strain the spaghetti
  • Turn off the heat to the skillet, wait 30 seconds
  • add the spaghetti, then the sauce from the bowl to the skillet, stir.
  • Now serve with extra pecorino (or parmegiano), and more pepper.
